HERNA JAAM!

Event Details

Hack The Circle: Khwopa 2024 is Bhaktapur's student-led hackathon, bringing together innovators, developers, and tech enthusiasts for a 32-hour coding marathon.

The vision of the event is to foster a culture of creativity, collaboration, and innovation within the tech community, nurturing the next generation of problem solvers.

It is open to students with a team of 2 to 4 members from any of the institutions of Khwopa circle.
